About OpenIndiana

OpenIndiana is a continuation of the OpenSolaris project, providing a free and open-source operating system based on the Illumos kernel. It is designed for developers, sysadmins, and enthusiasts who value stability, security, and advanced features.

The project is maintained by a global community of volunteers and is always looking for new contributors. Whether you're interested in coding, documentation, or testing, your help is welcome!

For more information, visit the official OpenIndiana website.
